# Dao De Jing — Translation

A translation of the Dao De Jing (道德經) into modern English.

## Goals
- Produce a faithful yet accessible English rendering of the DDJ
- Apply the terminology and style conventions from the broader translation project
- Preserve poetic rhythm and philosophical depth where possible

## Sources

- **@projects/dao/translation/ddj/jxz.md** — Jiang Xizhang (江希張) 1919 annotation + English translation. Chapters 1–10 complete. Published at https://books.tongde.us/books/ddj-jxz.
- **@projects/dao/translation/ddj/jxz-chinese.md** — Full original Chinese text (Simplified), all 81 chapters. Imported from `新注道德经白话解说——江希张.doc`. Structure per chapter: 原文 / 字解 / 章解 / 演說.
