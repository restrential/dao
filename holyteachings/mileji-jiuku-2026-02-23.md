### Allusion Notes

**拈花印證 (niān huā yìn zhèng)**
*Source:* 禪宗公案 (*Chan / Zen Koan*), the "拈花微笑" (Flower Sermon) story.
At Vulture Peak, Śākyamuni Buddha held up a flower before the assembly without speaking. Only Mahākāśyapa understood and smiled. The Buddha then transmitted to him the "Treasury of the True Dharma Eye" — the origin of the Chan school's mind-to-mind transmission beyond words.
In the Maitreya Sutra, "拈花印證考三乘" re-contextualizes this elite moment: Maitreya will use this wordless proof to test and validate all levels of spiritual attainment across the Three Vehicles, not merely the highest adepts.

**三乘 (sān chéng)**
*Source:* 妙法蓮華經 (*Saddharma Puṇḍarīka Sūtra / Lotus Sutra*).
The Three Vehicles (Śrāvaka, Pratyekabuddha, Bodhisattva) represent progressive stages toward enlightenment. The *Lotus Sutra*'s burning-house parable teaches that these are skillful means, ultimately all converging into One Vehicle.
In this text, "testing the Three Vehicles" signals that Maitreya's dharma will assess and unify all spiritual paths.

**天真收圓 (tiān zhēn shōu yuán)**
*Source:* 一貫道 (I-Kuan Tao) and related syncretic religious texts.
"天真" refers to the holy patriarchs administering salvation. "收圓" is the eschatological gathering — the final cosmic completion when all original souls are brought back to Heaven.
The phrase signals that the era of final universal salvation has begun under Maitreya's authority.

**混元 (hùn yuán)**
*Source:* 道德經 (*Tao Te Ching*), 淮南子 (*Huainanzi*), and other Daoist cosmological texts.
"Hunyuan" denotes the primordial, undifferentiated unity preceding all creation — the Dao before differentiation into yin and yang.
"混元古策在中央" links this scripture's salvation plan to primordial cosmic authority, suggesting the plan was embedded in the universe from its very origin.

**對合同 (duì hé tong)**
*Source:* Folk religious and secret society practices.
A "合同" was a tally, token, mudra, or secret sign used by initiates to confirm their covenant with a divine authority. "對合同" is the act of presenting this proof at the moment of divine judgment.
The annotation on this text specifies the hand gesture at the middle dantian (子亥抱在中丹田), identifying "合同" as the initiatic mudra received at transmission (點道).

**點鐵化成金 (diǎn tiě huà chéng jīn)**
*Source:* 道教煉丹術 (Daoist Alchemy), 抱朴子 (*Baopuzi*).
Literally "touch iron and transform it to gold" — the alchemist's supreme feat as metaphor for transforming the base into the perfected.
"鄉兒點鐵化成金" democratizes this power: ordinary people who recognize the White Era teacher can achieve total transformation of their nature.

**三災八難 (sān zāi bā nàn)**
*Source:* Common term in Buddhist and Daoist scriptures.
The Three Calamities (fire, flood, wind at cosmic end-cycles, or war, plague, famine) and Eight Difficulties (conditions preventing one from hearing the Dharma). A comprehensive formula for all forms of suffering.
The promise that "三災八難不來侵" offers complete protection — physical, spiritual, and cosmic — to the devoted chanter.

**牢栓意馬 (láo shuān yì mǎ)**
*Source:* Variation of "心猿意馬" (xīn yuán yì mǎ) from Han apocrypha and Buddhist transformation texts (變文).
The "monkey-mind and horse-will" — the restless, uncontrolled mind. "Firmly tying the horse-will" is an instruction to actively subdue discursive thought as a prerequisite for the advanced practice of contemplating "non-birth" (無生, anutpāda).

**三官大帝 (sān guān dà dì)**
*Source:* Daoist canonical pantheon.
The Three Great Emperor-Officials who govern Heaven, Earth, and Water — granting blessings, absolving sins, averting calamities. Their inclusion here signals that the entire Daoist divine bureaucracy is co-participating in Maitreya's work of salvation.

**北方真武 (běi fāng zhēn wǔ)**
*Source:* Daoist canonical pantheon; Ming dynasty cult of 玄天上帝.
The True Warrior of the North — lord of the element Water, supreme demon-queller, depicted with black banner and unbound hair. The Ming emperors attributed military successes to his protection.
Cast here as Maitreya's supreme general, he brings full martial authority against all forces of obstruction.

**白陽 (bái yáng)**
*Source:* 羅教 (Luo Jiao), 一貫道 (I-Kuan Tao), and related salvationist scriptures.
Part of the three-era framework: Green Era (Dīpaṃkara Buddha) → Red Era (Śākyamuni Buddha) → White Era (Maitreya Buddha). The White Era is the last — the age of universal salvation, accessible to all.
"白陽子" and "轉到三陽彌勒尊" confirm this scripture's eschatological setting: the White Era has arrived.

**嬰兒奼女 (yīng'ér chà nǚ)**
*Source:* 道教內丹術 (Daoist Neidan), 參同契 (*Cantong Qi / Seal of the Unity of the Three*).
In internal alchemy, the Infant symbolizes the Yang spirit / true nature; the Maiden symbolizes the Yin essence / life-force. Their union produces the golden elixir. Here, these terms are repurposed to refer to pure-hearted male and female devotees whose constant chanting affords them spiritual protection.

**水火既濟 (shuǐ huǒ jì jì)**
*Source:* 易經 (*I Ching*), Hexagram 63 — Water over Fire.
When heart-fire descends and kidney-water rises (the reverse of ordinary flow), perfect inner harmony is achieved — the pivotal internal alchemy transformation. Patriarch Lü's closing couplet directly invokes this principle as the ultimate realization of the Dao.

## Research Summary — Gleaned Insights

- **White Era eschatology is the organizing framework:** Every major divine figure, cosmic event, and salvific promise in this text is organized around the Three-Era cosmology. "White Era" must be rendered consistently; avoid "White Sun" or "White Yang."
- **Established terminology applies broadly:** 彌勒 = Maitreya (leave untranslated or use "Maitreya Buddha"); 老母 / 天花老母 = Heavenly Mother; 鄉兒 / 原人 = "children of the homeland" or "original souls" (context-dependent); 白陽子 = "son of the White Era."
- **天真 in 天真收圓:** In the I-Kuan Tao context, this refers to the holy patriarchs (師尊/師母 level) who administer cosmic salvation — not a generic "heavenly purity." Render as "the Holy Ones" or leave with parenthetical gloss.
- **合同 is the initiatic mudra:** The webpage annotation confirms "合同" refers to the specific hand gesture (子亥 position at the middle dantian) received at Dao transmission. Render as "the sacred covenant seal" or "the initiatic seal" rather than "contract."
- **嬰兒奼女 as devotees, not alchemy:** This text repurposes the neidan vocabulary to refer to devoted male and female practitioners. Translate as "the pure-hearted disciples" or keep the metaphorical language with a brief note.
- **Patriarch Lü's couplet turns inward:** The closing verse shifts the entire text's register — from cosmic drama to inner cultivation. Fire descending and Water rising is the internal alchemical realization, not an external event. This inward turn is the climax; the translation must honor it.
- **無皇 vs 吾皇:** The annotated version of the text reads 吾皇 (My Sovereign — i.e., Heavenly Mother). The verse "吾皇勒令寄下生" = "Heavenly Mother issues the imperial decree, entrusting [the mission] to one who descends into birth." This refers to the Patriarch's divine mandate.

## Step 3: Smoothed Translation

# The True Sutra of Maitreya's Salvation from Suffering

*As spoken by the Buddha:*

The Sutra of Maitreya Saving from Suffering —
Maitreya's descent to this world is no small matter.
He receives the sacred treasure at the holy mountains of Shandong,
Taking up the flower [of Chan transmission] to certify and test all Three Vehicles.
He appears in the Central Plains, in the land of the Three Stars,
Fully verified at Wang Tao Xin in Sichuan.
The Holy Ones gather the circle to completion and proclaim the sacred name,
Waiting for the appointed hour to marshal the divine soldiers.

Clouds and thunder shake open the central earth,
And throughout all under heaven, spirits and ghosts are uneasy.
Personally present in the benevolent heaven — the Heavenly Mother of the Chinese lands —
The Nine-Lotus Holy Teaching returns to the Supreme Vehicle.
The Heavenly Flower Venerable Mother lowers the jade thread of salvation,
Her gathering and transformation manifest in the ancient East.
North and south, the two poles are bound in a single lineage;
The primordial plan of Hunyuan stands at the center.

Heavenly Mother sends down the opening that leads to Heaven.
Before the Shadowless Mountain, present the sacred covenant seal.
You who long to return home —
Uphold and recite the Coming Maitreya Sutra.
Recite it with your whole heart, and the Buddha comes to save.
Every golden lotus rises above the cycle of rebirth.

Recognize the Son of the White Era who comes from the West.
The children of the homeland — touched, iron becomes gold.
Every day, with a resolved heart, constantly recite and uphold [this sutra].
The Three Calamities and Eight Difficulties shall not intrude.
If you wish to attain Buddhahood, bow in diligent devotion.
Always hold the heart of bright wisdom.
Do not listen to deviant voices and their slander.
Firmly tether the unruly mind and contemplate the unborn.

Heavenly Mother sends down the true celestial mantra.
Recite it with your whole heart — divine powers will arise.
All the stars of heaven descend to this world.
The immortals of the Five Directions come down from the Heavenly Palace.
The City Gods of every region come to verify their rosters.
The Spirit Messenger Boys investigate with clear sight.
The Three Great Emperor-Officials compassionately inscribe the record.
The Heavenly Bureau pardons sins and saves all sentient beings.
The Celestial Worthy Who Saves from Suffering comes to redeem the world,
Personally appointing the Divine Messengers of the Literary Bureau.

The Eight Great Vajras come to protect the Dharma.
The Four Bodhisattvas save sentient beings.
Thirty-six generals march in close formation.
Five hundred divine officers follow at their heels —
All supporting Maitreya in accomplishing the Great Dao,
Protecting and blessing the children of the homeland with peace.

The True Warrior of the North commands as supreme general.
Blue-green of face, red of hair — his divine powers shine forth.
He raises the black banner to blot out the sun and moon.
Atop his crown blazes the vast array of the Seven Treasure Stars.
His might commands the North; he is the commander-in-chief.
His armored troops stand ready to sweep clean all evil.
He comes to rescue the original souls, the sons and daughters of the homeland.
The fire-light falls to earth and is transformed to dust.

The Dragon Kings of the Four Seas come to support Dao,
Each riding auspicious clouds and ascending through the sky.
The divine troops of the ten directions guard the Buddha's procession,
Protecting and blessing Maitreya as he accomplishes his mission.

The Red Era has completed its Dao and returned home.
The age now turns to the honored Maitreya — the Third Sun Era.
Heavenly Mother issues her decree, entrusted to one who descends into birth,
To subdue the southern fires and return all to the true lineage.
He goes forth and forges the true mantra,
Transmitting the Great Treasury of Sutras for the age of Maitreya to come.

Male and female disciples — constantly uphold and recite.
Evil spirits dare not draw near.
Recite once — great divine power arises.
Recite twice — you will transcend the cycle of rebirth.
Recite three times — spirits and ghosts stand in awe,
And all demons are transformed to dust.
Cultivating through the age of tribulation, seek the path.
Arise in recitation of the true mantra and return to the Buddha's command.

*Nāmo Tiānyuán Tàibǎo — Amitābha Buddha — ten prostrations.*
